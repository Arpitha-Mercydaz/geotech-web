About Us

GEOTECH TELEMATICS INDIA PRIVATE LIMITED is a trusted provider of advanced connectivity solutions designed for next-generation telematics and IoT ecosystems.

We specialize in providing high-performance SIM and eSIM solutions designed specifically for Vehicle Tracking (VTL) systems and connected devices. Our services ensure seamless communication, real-time data transmission, and uninterrupted connectivity across multiple network environments.

With a strong foundation in innovation and reliability, we empower businesses to optimize operations, enhance fleet visibility, and improve decision-making through advanced connectivity infrastructure.

Our solutions are built to support large-scale deployments, offering flexibility, scalability, and robust performance for enterprises operating in dynamic and demanding environments.
