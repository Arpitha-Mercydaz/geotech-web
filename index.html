About Us

GEOTECH TELEMATICS INDIA PRIVATE LIMITED is a Kerala-based Private Limited company, registered on 14-Nov-2022 with the Ministry of Corporate Affairs (MCA).

We specialize in delivering advanced SIM and eSIM connectivity solutions for Vehicle Tracking (VTL) and IoT devices. Our platform ensures seamless network coverage, reliable communication, and scalable infrastructure for telematics businesses.

With a strong focus on innovation and performance, we empower enterprises with smart connectivity solutions that drive efficiency and growth.
